St. Theresa's Business & Technology Training Institute is a Private Institution in the Arch-diocese of Nyeri. The day-to-day management of the Institution is vested by the Sisters of Mary Immaculate of Nyeri. The College is Registered by Technical and Vocational Training Authority (TVET) and approved by KNEC as an Examination center of Business & Technical Courses.
