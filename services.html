About Our Tea:

What is St. Theresa's Business & Technology Training Institute?

St. Theresa's Business & Technology Training Institute s a leading institute of higher learning focused on the intersection of business and technology. We offer Artisan, Craft Certificate, Short Courses, Kasneb and Diploma Courses programs that equip students with the skills and knowledge to thrive in today's dynamic world.

Where is your institute located?

It is Situated along Nyeri-Ihururu road, 100m from the Nyeri Boys High School Junction and 500m from the Consolata Mathari Hospital.

What are your core values?

Our core values are innovation, collaboration, excellence and integrity. We strive to foster a learning environment that encourages creativity, teamwork, and ethical leadership.

What makes your Institute unique?

We combine a rigorous business curriculum with cutting-edge technology education, preparing students to become business leaders who understand technology and technologists who comprehend the business landscape.

Do you have student housing?

Yes, we offer on-campus housing options for undergraduate students.

What kind of student activities do you have?

We have a vibrant campus life with a wide range of student clubs and organizations catering to academic, social, cultural, and professional interests.

Do you offer career services?

Our career services department provides students with career counseling, interview preparation, resume writing workshops, and job placement assistance.

What are the athletic facilities like?

We have a modern athletic complex with state-of-the-art facilities for various sports and fitness activities. We also offer intramural sports leagues for students to participate in.

Our campus features state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, labs, libraries, and student centers. We offer a vibrant and welcoming environment.
