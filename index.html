Who we are

We will provide you the best work skills & Essentials which you dreamt for!

We invite you to indulge in the extraordinary and to become part of a legacy in the making. St. Theresa's Business & Technology Training Institute. -Kenya is an award winning private institute in the Archidiocese of Nyeri, located along the Nyeri - Ihururu road, just 100m from the Nyeri Boys High School Junction. The day-to-day Management of the Institution is vested by the Sisters of Mary Immaculate of Nyeri. This College is Registered by the Technical and Vocational Training Authority(TVET), and Approved by KNEC as an examination center of Business & Technical Courses. With our unique processes, we deliver distinctive young men & women in the job market that result in a remarkable 98% satisfaction rating. With each Student, it is understood that this is not about simply equipping with the working skills — it is about manifesting the vision of the student and keeping that vision the central focus throughout the course duration.
